Everything in one place.

From your first session of the season to the post-race debrief — Contoura keeps every detail organised and ready to act on.

01

Training Log

Record every session — distance, elevation, map, and a 7-dimension quality rating covering technique, physical, mental, and more.

02

Competition Analysis

Import your race results and get an instant deep dive — split-by-split breakdowns, performance index, error density, and quarter-course statistics.

03

Race Preparation

Define your goals, upload course documents, note the race centre, and build a pre-race schedule — all linked to a single competition entry.

04

Statistics Dashboard

Monthly distance trends, rating developments, ranking progressions, training streaks — visualised and updated automatically, no manual work.

05

Training Planner

Drag-and-drop monthly calendar with colour-coded session types, weekly load targets, and an actual vs. planned comparison view.

06

Livelox Integration

Attach GPS track links to any training or race entry and review your route choices directly alongside your written evaluation.
