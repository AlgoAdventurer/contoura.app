3 The Service

Contoura is an all-in-one performance platform built specifically for orienteering athletes and coaches. The Service currently provides the following core features:

  • Training Log — record sessions with distance, elevation, map, and a 7-dimension quality rating covering technical, physical, mental, and other aspects of performance.
  • Competition Analysis — import race results in IOF XML format and receive automated split-by-split breakdowns, performance index, error density, and quarter-course statistics.
  • Race Preparation — define goals, upload course documents, note the race centre, and build a structured pre-race schedule, all linked to a single competition entry.
  • Statistics Dashboard — visualise monthly distance trends, quality rating developments, ranking progressions, and training streaks, updated automatically.
  • Training Planner — drag-and-drop monthly calendar with colour-coded session types, weekly load targets, and an actual-versus-planned comparison view.
  • Livelox Integration — attach GPS track links to any training or race entry and review route choices alongside written evaluations.

The Service is currently in closed beta. Access is granted by invitation only. Once publicly launched, it will be offered as a freemium platform: core features free of charge, with optional paid plans for premium functionality. You will be clearly informed of any costs before being charged.
